# Autonomous Revenue OS™
### Who To Hire On Fiverr & What It Costs

*Prepared for: Elizabeth Rothschild*
*Date: June 21, 2026*

> Plain-language plan. Prices are typical **Fiverr ranges** — you can often find good people at the lower end. Spend top-to-bottom: do the **"Start Here"** group first, add the rest as money comes in.

---

## How to think about this

You do **not** need to hire everyone at once. There are three tiers:

1. **Start Here** — the few people who make the biggest difference right away.
2. **Grow** — add these once the first customers/cash start coming in.
3. **Scale** — for when it's working and you want to pour fuel on the fire.

---

## TIER 1 — START HERE (the essentials)

These get you sending real messages, looking professional, and taking payments.

| # | Who to hire (search this on Fiverr) | What they do for you | Typical cost |
|---|--------------------------------------|----------------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Twilio / A2P 10DLC registration expert** | Gets your texting account approved so messages actually deliver instead of getting blocked. **This is your current roadblock — fix it first.** | $75 – $250 |
| 2 | **Sales copywriter (SMS & email)** | Writes the persuasive messages that make people reply and buy. | $50 – $200 |
| 3 | **Brand / logo & identity designer** | Polishes your logo, colors, and look so you appear trustworthy and premium. | $50 – $300 |
| 4 | **Stripe / payment setup specialist** | Makes sure "tap to pay" works flawlessly and money lands in your account. | $40 – $150 |

**Tier 1 total: about $215 – $900**

## Advertising budget (separate from people)

Ads cost money *on top of* the person who runs them. A realistic starting point:

- **Test budget:** $300 – $500 / month
- **Growth budget:** $1,000 – $3,000 / month

Start small, see what works, then increase.

---

| Goal | One-time setup | Monthly ongoing |
|------|---------------|-----------------|

## My honest recommendation

**Start with just $300 – $500 and hire Tier 1, #1 and #2 first:**

1. The **Twilio/A2P expert** — because right now your texts won't send to strangers until that's done.
2. The **sales copywriter** — because the right words are what turn a text into a sale.

Get those two done, send your first real campaign, and let the early income pay for the next hires. **You can build this whole thing one paid step at a time.**
